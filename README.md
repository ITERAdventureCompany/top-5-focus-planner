# Top 5 Focus Planner website

Official website, privacy policy, terms of service, and support pages for Top 5 Focus Planner.

## Files

- `index.html` — product landing page
- `privacy.html` — privacy policy
- `terms.html` — terms of service
- `support.html` — support and FAQ
- `styles.css` — shared design
- `app-icon.png` — app icon, favicon, and social preview

## Publish with GitHub Pages

1. Upload all files to the root of this repository.
2. Open **Settings → Pages**.
3. Under **Build and deployment**, choose **Deploy from a branch**.
4. Select the `main` branch and `/ (root)`.
5. Save and wait for GitHub Pages to publish.

The site URL should look like:

`https://iteradventurecompany.github.io/top-5-focus-planner/`

## Before launch

Verify that these statements still match the app:

- No account or login
- No analytics or crash-reporting SDK
- No advertising or cross-app tracking
- Task content remains local to the device
- Reminders are local notifications
- Purchases are processed by Apple

Update the policies whenever the app’s data practices or monetization change.

> These template documents are practical publishing materials, not individualized legal advice.
